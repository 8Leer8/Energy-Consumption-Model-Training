What is Energy Consumption Predictor?

Energy Consumption Predictor is a web-based tool that leverages advanced machine learning to forecast building energy usage. It is designed for both professionals and everyday users who want actionable insights into their energy consumption patterns.

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to empower users to make data-driven decisions, optimize energy usage, reduce operational costs, and contribute to a more sustainable future.

Uses

  • Forecast monthly or daily energy consumption for buildings and facilities
  • Identify trends and patterns in energy usage
  • Support budgeting and resource planning
  • Monitor the impact of energy-saving initiatives
  • Educate users on efficient energy practices

Advantages

  • Easy-to-use, intuitive interface for all users
  • Interactive dashboard for visual insights
  • Supports both business and personal energy management
  • Helps reduce costs and environmental impact
